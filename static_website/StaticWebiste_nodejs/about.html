Our Values

Principles that
guide everything

01

Relentless Precision

We ship nothing we wouldn't stake our reputation on. Every model, every API, every line of code is scrutinized until it's exactly right. Good enough is never good enough.

02

Human-Centered AI

Technology serves people — never the other way around. We design systems that augment human capability, preserve human judgment, and protect human dignity.

03

Open Collaboration

The best ideas don't come from silos. We work transparently with clients, contribute to open research, and build partnerships grounded in mutual respect and shared ambition.

Our Journey

From a small
lab to a global force

We started as a research group at IIT Bengaluru. Today we operate across 12 countries, with products that serve millions of users daily.

2019
Founded as TechLoka Research
Three researchers, one rented lab, and one very ambitious idea about the future of AI.
2020
First Product Launch
Released TL-Chat, our first conversational AI — 50,000 users in 30 days.
2022
Series A · $18M
Backed by Sequoia India and Tiger Global. Expanded to cloud AI and cybersecurity verticals.
2024
Global Expansion
Offices in Bengaluru, Singapore, London, and San Francisco. 200+ enterprise clients worldwide.
2025
TechLoka AI v3 Platform
Our most powerful release yet — a unified AI platform for enterprise intelligence at any scale.
